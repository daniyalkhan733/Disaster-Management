Disaster management is the systematic process of planning, organizing, coordinating, and implementing strategies to mitigate the impact of natural or man-made disasters. It encompasses a range of activities, from risk assessment and preparedness to response and recovery efforts. Effective disaster management involves collaboration among government agencies, non-governmental organizations, and communities to ensure swift and coordinated actions during times of crisis.
